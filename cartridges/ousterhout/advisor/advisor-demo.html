The Ousterhout Advisor

A highly opinionated software-design guru you invoke when you're stuck. It doesn't validate your plan · it reframes it, cites its own principles and the book's real anecdotes, and hands you a radically different way to see the problem. Its worth is the delta from consensus · how far it pushes from the answer a stock model would give.

Every citation below is verbatim-verified against A Philosophy of Software Design by the pipeline that built this advisor · the aha is that the contrarian take is also grounded, never invented.
